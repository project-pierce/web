Our Food For Your Thought

About Project Pierce

Project Pierce is a website project created with the intent of giving Stevens students the ability to review and create recipes based on foods offered at Pierce in the hopes of getting a little more bang out of your meal plan.

How to Use the Site

Find recipes for the type of food you want to make or find at Pierce by clicking on the respective button on the nav bar.

Add Your Own Recipes!

Just visit our Recipe Maker to submit your own recipe for us to spread to the Stevens community!

Website created by CS146D group 9, A1, consisting of team members Madison Gaudet, Tyler Kaminski, Lasya Josyula, Anushtup Bhowmik, and Cindy Zhang.
